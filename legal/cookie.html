Cookie Policy

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us improve your experience. They allow us to remember your preferences and analyze how our site is used to provide you with the best catering experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use essential cookies for login and security (keeping your sessions safe), and performance cookies to understand which curries and features are most popular among our foodie community.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party analytics (like Google Analytics) that set cookies on our behalf to help us measure site performance and user behavior, allowing us to optimize our delivery routes and service quality.

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may limit your ability to use some premium features of UNIV Foods, such as tracking your order in real-time.

5. Questions?

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please reach out to: tech@univfoods.in
